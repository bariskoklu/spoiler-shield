# Privacy Policy — Spoiler Shield

**Last updated: March 2026**

Spoiler Shield does not collect, transmit, or share any user data.

## What we store

Your settings (keywords, blocked channels, and toggle states) are saved locally on your device using Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.local` API. This data never leaves your device.

## What we do not do

- We do not collect any personal information
- We do not track your browsing activity
- We do not send any data to external servers
- We do not use analytics or third-party services
- We do not use cookies

## How it works

Spoiler Shield runs entirely in your browser. It reads YouTube page content to detect matching video titles and channel names, then hides them locally. No information about the videos you see or hide is recorded or transmitted anywhere.

## Contact

If you have any questions, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/bariskoklu/spoiler-shield).
